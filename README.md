# NUS Future Fabrication Lab

A 6-month internship project with the NUS Future Fabrication Lab, focused on digital fabrication and robotics research.

## About

The Future Fabrication Lab explores the intersection of digital fabrication, robotic construction, and computational tools — including CNC machining, 3D printing, and robotic arm-driven manufacturing processes. This repository tracks work carried out during the internship, including code, experiments, and documentation produced along the way.

## Goals

- Explore and prototype digital fabrication workflows
- Develop tooling/scripts to support robotic fabrication processes
- Document experiments, findings, and progress over the internship period
